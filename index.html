Who we are?

We are a group of coders meeting regularly and working through a variety of subjects involving mainly software development. If you're interested into meeting friendly people sharing your passion, just join in one of our sessions.

Our Goals

Continuous Improvement

Are you unsatisfied with your current mastery at your craftsmanship? That's good! We are too. We are seeking to improve ourselves continuously, and that's why we're here.

Networking

You feel alone or alienated when you talk your peers about TDD? Want to meet people alike? You got an idea? There is a great chance you'll meet some people you'll enjoy talking to.

Sharing

You know what they say. You don't get to learn a subject until unless you can explain it to a six years old. We are ready to listen. Be an expert or a beginner. It really doesn't matter.

Creating together

This one's more of a wish then something we do. We expect to join forces at some point and creating something of value for the community. Help us define it.
